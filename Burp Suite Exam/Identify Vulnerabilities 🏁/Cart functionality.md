## _**Cart functionality**_
- Excessive trust in client-side controls 
	- (change the price of an item manually)
- High-level logic vulnerability 
	- (add negative values of products to eventually reach a range within the available store credit after adding a product)
- Insufficient workflow validation 
	- (the app confirms the purchase to all the items in cart without checking the store credit )
- Low-level logic flaw
	- ( add too many products to loop back around to the minimum possible value to eventually reach a range within the available store credit after adding a product)
- Exploiting a mass assignment vulnerability
